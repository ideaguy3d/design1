INSTRUCTIONS

1st Step:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line baking sheets with parchment paper, and set aside. In a large bowl, combine flour, oats, sugar, and coconut. Set aside.

2nd Step:

In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt butter with syrup. Dissolve baking soda in boiling water, and add to butter mixture.

3rd Step:

Stir to combine. (Be careful; if the butter is hot, it will bubble up considerably)

4th Step:

Add butter mixture to dry ingredients, and stir to combine. Using a 1 1/2-inch ice-cream scoop, drop onto prepared baking sheets, about 2 inches apart (be sure to pack the scoop tightly so the mixture doesn't crumble). Flatten biscuits slightly with the heel of your hand.

5th Step:

Bake until golden brown and firm but not hard, about 15 minutes. Transfer to wire racks to cool and then enjoy!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup McKenzies Desiccated Coconut
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons CSR Golden Syrup
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
