International Orders

!!! PLEASE NOTE !!!
WE DO NOT SHIP FOOD, COSMETIC, OR MEDICINAL ITEMS OF ANY KIND TO ANY
INTERNATIONAL DESTINATIONS OTHER THAN CANADA.

Shipping Rates for ALL International orders including Canada are determined on an order by order basis. Customers will be contacted with the applicable shipping rates prior to orders being confirmed.

Duty, Clearance Fees, Customs Fees, etc. are the sole responsibility of the customer. Australian Products Co. cannot determine these charges in advance and will not be responsible for these charges. Customs documents are mandatory on each shipment. These documents will be printed and attached to your shipment by Australian Products Co. at a fee of $7.95   As a note, packages to any country other than America are subject to Duty and other such fees.

International Orders:  All products have a 30 day retention prior to dispatch excluding Canada.
