Quiet Buddy, 
Welcome to what started in my little garage in 1989, and has become my proudest life's work, next to my son Taylor, of course.

Here at Australian Products Co., we've proudly been "Bringing Australia to You" and will continue to do so in the years to come thanks to our talented (and often zany) staff below:

It really is a pleasure to work with a fantastic team!

Dawn - Down Under Delights Spokesmodel

Aussie "Dawn", our promoter for the Down Under Delights line of frozen foods here at Australian Products Company, was named in honour of legendary Aussie swimmer Dawn Fraser! Christyne swam for many years in her youth and always idolized Dawn Fraser's Olympic achievements, from her gold medals in Melbourne to her medals in Tokyo. For us, Dawn represents everything Australian. She has that fantastic Aussie spirit, that sense of honour to history, and that pioneering excitement for the future.
   

Linda - Accounting & Bookkeeping

Linda comes to us on loan from Stanford where she does amazing things she never tells us about. Frankly, she often just sneaks in shortly after we leave, cranks up the radio, and dances about while doing the books and paperwork.
