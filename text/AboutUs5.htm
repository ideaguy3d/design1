​About Us -


Since 1989 Australian Products Co. have specialized in "Bringing Australia To You." With over 4,500 products, we supply the expat community with the comforts of "home" and the American market with a "taste" of Australia (Souvenirs, Australian food, Akubra Hats, Driza-Bone coats, Educational Material, Australian Meat Pies and Sausage Rolls are just a few of our lines). Yes, we do stock the famous Vegemite!

Australian Products Co. has been featured on Evening Magazine, Eye On The Bay, 7 News, CBS series Survivor, in the San Francisco Chronicle and Wall Street Journal. 

http://www.wsj.com/articles/country-specific-stores-the-next-best-thing-to-buying-there-1405090420

We have continued to grow our business and expand our product line. In February 2015, we moved to our current location at 3828 Stevens Creek Blvd in San Jose. We've been experiencing growing exposure at our new venue as we are continuing to provide excellent service to Aussies, British and friends everywhere.  Many of our products are shipped worldwide. In February 2012 we expanded our stock line to include "Best of the British". On behalf of all the staff at Australian Products Co., I would like to thank you for your valued and continued support. We look forward to continuing our goal of "Bringing Australia To You".
